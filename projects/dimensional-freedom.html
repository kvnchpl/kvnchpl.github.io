*Dimensional Freedom*

May 2019

Though distinct from the physical world, we can interact with virtual spaces with the same familiarity and utility as real ones. This installation encapsulates the fluidity of a virtual space, urging the viewer to weave through it – an experience that straddles the second and third dimensions. The figures onscreen are also stuck in a 2d space while receding into and escaping from their planes. The track on which they walk facilitates a perpetual movement through time and a perpetual shuffling of identity. The space between the pillars thus becomes unresolvable, destabilizing one’s orientation as in virtual space.
