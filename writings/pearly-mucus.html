*Pearly Mucus*

December 2023

pearly mucus piles up in the corner of the room
"i've seen worse, and i will see much worse,"
they think to themselves separately.
this place has become a damp cemetery—
she notices yet another tear drip from
her lover's chin through the reflection of a steak knife.
"will it always be this way?"
