*Flex Space*

August 2025

bright track lights glaring
in my eyes, i close
them as h continues
the body scan.
i feel my nape and the very back
of my head especially
against the dark gray
foam mat curled slightly
at the other end.
we go into warrior one, two, three:
inhale, hold, exhale.
are everyone else's hamstrings
as tight as mine?
my crotch starts to smell
through black mesh shorts.
        
(contribution to insular circular zine, issue 1)
